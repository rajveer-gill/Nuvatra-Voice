# Setting Up Your AI Receptionist (Call Forwarding)

You keep your existing business number. We give you a second number (your "AI line"),
and you tell your phone carrier to send incoming calls to it. Customers keep calling the
same number they always have — the AI just answers behind the scenes.

**Your AI line:** `__________________` (we'll fill this in for you)

---

## Step 1 — Choose how calls forward

- **Forward ALL calls** → the AI answers every call. Best if the AI is replacing your front desk.
- **Forward only when you don't pick up** → your phone rings first; the AI catches missed,
  busy, and after-hours calls. Best if you still answer some calls yourself.

Pick one, then follow your carrier below.

---

## Step 2 — Turn on forwarding

### Mobile phones

**iPhone (AT&T, T-Mobile, and most carriers):**
Settings → Phone → Call Forwarding → toggle ON → enter your AI line.
*(Note: this is "forward all calls." Verizon iPhones use the star codes below instead.)*

**Android:**
Phone app → ⋮ menu → Settings → Calls → Call forwarding → "Always forward" → enter your AI line.

### Star codes (landlines, VoIP desk phones, Verizon mobile)

Dial these from your business phone:

| Action | Dial | Then |
|---|---|---|
| Forward ALL calls | `*72` | enter your AI line, press call, wait for the confirmation tone |
| Forward when busy | `*90` | enter your AI line |
| Forward when no answer | `*92` | enter your AI line |
| **Turn OFF forwarding** | `*73` | press call |

*(Codes are the same on most US carriers. If `*72` doesn't work, check your carrier's app.)*

### Internet phone services (RingCentral, Grasshopper, Google Voice, Vonage, etc.)

These don't use star codes — log into your provider's app or website and look for
**Call Forwarding** or **Call Handling** settings, then point calls to your AI line.

---

## Step 3 — Test it

1. Call your **normal business number** from a different phone.
2. The AI should answer.
3. Book a test appointment — you should get a confirmation text.

That's it. To stop forwarding at any time, dial `*73` (or toggle it off in your settings).

---

**Heads up:** the confirmation text your customers receive will come from your AI line,
not your main number. That's normal — the message says your business name so they know it's you.
